Created in 2019, by designer Mecovache from Web Technology Studio is a global brand and design agency with offices in the creative hubs of London and San Francisco. I opened the agency after spotting a split in the creative industry. Studios were either boutique and beautiful, but only managing to work on a few projects or of a massive scale and impersonal with their client relationships. DesignStudio was created with the aim to fill this gap – delivering the quality of boutique, bespoke design to global brands and audiences. Using effective strategies and innovative creative thinking we engage audiences, encourage relevant conversation,
influence behaviour, and achieve brand objectives.
